  <h1>Awesome Warfarin Projects</h1>
  
  <p>A curated list of awesome open-source projects, research, and resources related to Warfarin therapy. Warfarin is a widely used anticoagulant prescribed to millions worldwide to prevent blood clots, but dosing can be challenging due to factors like genetics, diet, and interactions. These tools and projects aim to improve dosing accuracy, patient management, and research in pharmacogenomics.</p>

## üß† Machine Learning & Research

Projects using ML, data analysis, and statistical modeling to predict optimal warfarin dosage. These often leverage datasets like IWPC for personalized medicine.

## üìö Related Resources

Links to relevant datasets, research papers, articles, and communities.

- **Datasets**:
  - [IWPC-Warfarin-Dataset](https://www.pharmgkb.org/page/iwpc) - The International Warfarin Pharmacogenetics Consortium (IWPC) dataset, widely used for building predictive models.
  - [PharmGKB Warfarin Page](https://www.pharmgkb.org/chemical/PA451906) - Comprehensive pharmacogenomics data on Warfarin.

- **Research Papers**:
  - [Machine Learning for Warfarin Dosing in Diverse Populations (Frontiers in Pharmacology, 2021)](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fphar.2021.749786/full) - Related to the karneslab project.

- **Guidelines & Tools**:
  - [CPIC Guidelines for Warfarin](https://cpicpgx.org/guidelines/guideline-for-warfarin-and-cyp2c9-and-vkorc1/) - Clinical Pharmacogenetics Implementation Consortium recommendations.

- **Communities**:
  - [r/anticoagulants on Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/anticoagulants/) - Discussions for patients on Warfarin and similar therapies.
  - [PharmGKB Forums](https://www.pharmgkb.org/) - For researchers in pharmacogenomics.
